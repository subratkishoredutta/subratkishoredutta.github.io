I’m Subrat Kishore Dutta, a first year PhD student at CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security and University of Hamburg under the supervision of Dr. Xiao Zhang and Prof. Dr. Anne Lauscher. My research centres around Adversarial Machine Learning and its utility in understanding and improving Fairness of Foundational Models. I completed my MSc. in Informatik at the Universität des Saarlandes, where I published my Master's Thesis at ICCV 2025.
