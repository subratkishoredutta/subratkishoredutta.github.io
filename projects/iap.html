IAP: Invisible Adversarial Patch Attack

Authors: Subrat Kishore Dutta, Dr. Xiao Zhang

Conference: ICCV 2025

Paper Code

Abstract

We propose IAP, a perceptibility-aware framework for generating highly stealthy adversarial patches. Unlike existing approaches, our method optimizes both the perturbation and placement based on visual saliency and local contrast cues. We demonstrate that IAP is effective in digital and physical environments, while remaining imperceptible to humans.

Highlights

  • Perceptibility-aware patch generation & placement
  • Strong targeted attacks in both white-box and black-box settings
  • Transferable and physically realizable attacks
